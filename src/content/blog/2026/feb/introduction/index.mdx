## Personal Life
I'm currently an undergraduate student <a href="https://binus.ac.id/">Bina Nusantara University</a>, majoring in Computer Science. I like tech and stuff, but my personal life is beyond that. My first programming language was PHP, and I made some simple CRUD dynamic website with it (the time when I only knew about connection.php lol). Then I moved on to Java for my Android course, but hey I think Java sucks so I left it to dust lol. But there's no regret cause I learned OOP from there. Then I learned about Javascript, I really like it, and I made some cool projects with it along with ReactJS, NodeJS, and MongoDB.

time skip to my internship at a SaaS company (and some skips on my freelance projects),

### Being a "Musician" 🎵
I've been playing piano 🎹 since I was just a kid. It was classical piano. Man, it was really stressful for me to learn music theory and reading scores. But still no regret, all of my musicality trained from there. When I was in high school I started to learn to being an organist. In Indonesia, we barely use Orgel, real Pipe Organ, or digital Organ like Rodgers etc. We often use Yamaha Electone. I guess Electone is a lot easier than orgel or piano, electone has some life easing features like transposing. The keys are more smaller and lighter, easier to reach and press, unlike the real organ keys.

### Being a "Catholic" ✝️☦️
Rather than saying I'm that real Christian Catholic, I would say I'm a Christian who is still learning about the faith, and still trying to be a better Catholic. I was baptized when I was a baby, but I didn't really understand what it meant until I was in high school. I started to learn more about the faith, and I really like it. I like the apostolic concept, the teachings, the concept of love and God, and the other values that Catholicism teaches. I do still question some of the teachings, but I guess that's normal for a Christian, right? I'm still learning and growing in my faith, and I hope I can be a better Catholic in the future.

So I guess that's all for now. I'll be posting about more stuff in the future, like my projects, my thoughts about tech and life, and maybe even some music stuff. I hope you enjoy reading my blog.
