So I want to explore other fields in tech, like AI and machine learning, maybe even blockchain and Web3. Or who knows, maybe Network Engineering and DevOps?

I'm open to exploring and learning new things.

### Being a "Musician"

I've been playing piano since I was a kid. It started with classical piano.

Man, learning music theory and reading scores was really stressful. But I have no regrets because all of my musicality was built from that foundation.

When I was in high school, I started learning how to play the organ.

In Indonesia, we rarely use real pipe organs, digital organs like Rodgers, or traditional organs. Most churches use Yamaha Electone instead.

I guess Electone is much easier compared to piano or traditional organs because it has many convenient features, like transposing. The keys are also smaller and lighter, making them easier to reach and press compared to real organ keys.
