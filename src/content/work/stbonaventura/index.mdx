## Overview

A news and information portal developed for **St. Bonaventura Catholic Church** as part of a paid collaboration with the church’s **KOMSOS (Social Communication)** division and clergy members.

The platform was designed to centralize official church information, improve communication workflows, and support the church’s annual operational plans through a more accessible and structured digital presence.

## Responsibilities

- Designed and developed the platform end-to-end.
- Built a custom CMS tailored to the church’s existing administrative workflows and content management preferences.
- Structured the website architecture to support long-term maintainability and easier content publishing.
- Collaborated directly with church stakeholders, including KOMSOS members and clergy representatives.
- Developed responsive pages for:
  - Church profile
  - Church history
  - Parish services
  - Management and organizational information
  - Articles and announcements
  - News and event updates

## Impact

The platform provided a centralized and structured system for official church communication while improving public accessibility to parish information and activities.

Key outcomes included:
- Improved accessibility of church announcements and information
- Better organization of church content and operational updates
- Increased online visibility and parish exposure
- Easier long-term content management for internal church teams

<hr class="line" />

## Key Takeaways

This project emphasized:
- Building software around existing real-world organizational workflows
- Designing maintainable CMS systems for non-technical users
- Cross-functional collaboration with organizational stakeholders
- Creating scalable information systems for community-focused institutions
