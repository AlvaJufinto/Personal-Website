## Overview

Worked as a Frontend Engineer at Happy5 Pte. Ltd., contributing to the development and maintenance of enterprise-grade employee performance management systems used by large-scale companies across Indonesia.

The role focused on delivering reliable frontend solutions in a fast-paced environment, collaborating closely with product managers, customer success teams, designers, and backend engineers to ensure features and fixes could be delivered efficiently for enterprise clients.

---

## Responsibilities

- Collaborated directly with customer success and product teams to resolve production issues and critical bugs within short turnaround times, especially during high-pressure operational periods.
- Developed and improved core platform features related to:
  - Formal performance reviews
  - Talent analytics
  - Employee performance management
- Implemented optimization features such as CSV import processing for handling large datasets efficiently.
- Participated in sprint planning and cross-functional collaboration with frontend engineers, backend engineers, designers, and stakeholders.
- Contributed to feature delivery and maintenance for enterprise clients including:
  - BNI
  - BCA
  - Tiket.com
  - Biofarma
  - BSI

## Impact

The primary goal of the role was to maintain and improve Happy5’s User Performance Web Application while ensuring rapid and effective feature delivery for enterprise-scale clients.

Key contributions included:
- Improving workflow efficiency for large organizational datasets
- Supporting business continuity through fast bug resolution
- Enhancing performance review and analytics functionality
- Maintaining product stability during critical operational periods
