## Overview

Fide is an AI-powered catechism learning platform designed to help users understand, live, and defend the Catholic faith through structured and practical learning experiences.

The platform combines doctrinal education with interactive systems such as quizzes, debate simulations, gamification, and adaptive learning to encourage deeper understanding and active reasoning instead of passive memorization.

The project was developed as a collaborative initiative under the team **Deus Vult**.

<hr class="line" />

## Vision

To help Catholics build a deeper, more complete, and practically applicable understanding of the faith while remaining relevant in modern society.

<hr class="line" />

## Problem Statement

Many Catholics face challenges such as:
- Shallow understanding of doctrine
- Selective acceptance of Church teachings
- Influence of secularism and relativism
- Difficulty applying faith consistently in daily life

Fide was designed to address these issues through structured learning and active engagement.

## Core Features

### Structured Catechism Learning

Learning materials are organized based on the four primary pillars of Catholic doctrine:
- Creed
- Sacraments
- Moral Law
- Prayer

Content is presented using structured and definition-oriented explanations to reduce ambiguity.

### Quiz System

- Concept-based micro learning
- Real-time feedback
- Understanding validation and retention support

### AI Debate Simulation

Users can engage in simulated AI-assisted debates to:
- Practice defending beliefs logically
- Improve articulation and reasoning
- Strengthen doctrinal understanding

### Gamification

- Grace Points reward system
- Daily streak mechanics
- Anonymous leaderboard system
- Progress tracking and engagement systems

## Architecture & Development

The project follows a modular frontend architecture with:
- Reusable UI components
- Feature-based organization
- Context and Zustand-based state management
- Separation between API, hooks, layouts, and business logic

The platform was optimized for maintainability and rapid feature iteration during development.

## Impact

Fide demonstrates how interactive and AI-assisted systems can support faith formation and doctrinal education in a more engaging and structured way.

Key contributions included:
- Building scalable frontend systems
- Designing interactive learning workflows
- Translating theological learning structures into digital experiences
- Combining education, gamification, and AI-assisted reasoning within a single platform
