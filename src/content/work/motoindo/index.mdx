## Overview

MotoIndo required a modern and maintainable company website capable of supporting continuous content updates while improving the company’s online visibility and branding.

The project also included a dedicated landing page for their radio communication product, **Labra V22**, designed to present product information clearly and professionally for potential customers and partners.

---

## Responsibilities

- Led the project from technical planning to production deployment.
- Designed and implemented the frontend architecture using Next.js.
- Integrated Sanity Headless CMS to enable flexible and maintainable content management.
- Developed reusable frontend components for scalability and easier future updates.
- Optimized the website structure and metadata for better search visibility and SEO performance.
- Collaborated directly with stakeholders to align the website structure and content with business goals.

---

## Tech Stack

- Next.js
- React
- Sanity Headless CMS
- SEO Optimization

---

## Impact

The new MotoIndo website improved the company’s online presence by providing:
- A more modern and professional visual identity
- Easier long-term content management
- Better scalability for future updates
- Improved ranking for branded search queries

The project also demonstrated the effectiveness of combining static performance-focused frontend architecture with a flexible headless CMS workflow.

---

## Product Landing Page

One of the featured deliverables was the landing page for the **Labra V22** radio communication product, built to showcase:
- Product specifications
- Key features
- Company branding
- Marketing-focused presentation

The landing page emphasized clarity, responsiveness, and lightweight performance for broader accessibility across devices.
